How We Started

Elizabeth Graham and Jennifer Greive had a vision: to spread happiness by turning an everyday essential into a colorful design piece. In 2010, socks were just socks. White, gray and black pieces of textile that were nowhere close to the fashionable and creative design piece they could become. But that was all about to change.

SOXS, a high-quality sock that combines unique designs and craftsmanship. (Probably) The world’s most comfortable and colorful tool for spreading happiness was born. This concept is now brought to life by SOXS’ talented team of creatives.
