FAQ

Regrettably it is difficult to change your order or the shipping address once it has been placed. We aim to ship all orders as fast as possible so our warehouse is processing them within 15 minutes of receiving them. If a change is necessary please contact us and we will do our best to help you.
All orders are shipped with standard postal services from our warehouse in the Singapore. The delivery time depends on the destination country and you can find our country specific shipping overview here:

  • Shipping times Global (if your delivery address is located outside the European Union)
  • Shipping times EU (if your delivery address is located within the European Union)
Please keep in mind that all times are estimates and depend on the local postal service in your country.
We currently don't offer tracked shipping.
You can find the estimated delivery time for your order in your shipping confirmation email. Please note that it is calculated in business days (Mon-Fri). Since all orders are shipped by standard mail, the exact delivery time depends on your local postal service.

Please allow for the estimated delivery time plus a few extra business days (Mon-Fri) to pass. If your order does not arrive within the estimated time, it is most likely just a bit delayed but will still arrive within the next couple of days.
